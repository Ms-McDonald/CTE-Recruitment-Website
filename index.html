CREDIT/CERTIFICATION

The WDPP students will take an exam at the end of the program; this exam is provided by the National Occupational Competency Testing Institute (NOCTI). Successful completion of the NOCTI exam awards the students with an industry valued certification in Web Design.

Students enrolled in the WDPP are eligible to enroll to receive up to three (3) college credits from the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT).
